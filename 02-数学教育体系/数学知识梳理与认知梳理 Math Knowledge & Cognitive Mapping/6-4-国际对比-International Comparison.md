# 6.4 国际对比 International Comparison

## 小学初中数学国际主流标准核心理念与知识结构对比（中英双语）

### 1. 中国课程标准 Chinese Curriculum Standards

- 强调基础知识、基本技能和计算能力。
- Emphasizes basic knowledge, fundamental skills, and computational ability.
- 注重系统性、循序渐进和应试能力。
- Focuses on systematic learning, step-by-step progression, and exam skills.

### 2. IB（国际文凭）IB (International Baccalaureate)

- 强调探究式学习、跨学科联系和批判性思维。
- Emphasizes inquiry-based learning, interdisciplinary connections, and critical thinking.
- 注重概念理解和实际应用。
- Focuses on conceptual understanding and real-life application.

### 3. 剑桥体系 Cambridge System

- 注重知识结构、逻辑推理和全球视野。
- Focuses on knowledge structure, logical reasoning, and global perspective.
- 强调应用能力和创新素养。
- Emphasizes application skills and innovative literacy.

### 4. 美国共同核心标准 Common Core (USA)

- 强调数学实践、问题解决和推理能力。
- Emphasizes mathematical practice, problem solving, and reasoning skills.
- 注重理解过程和表达能力。
- Focuses on understanding processes and communication skills.

### 5. 共同点与差异 Commonalities & Differences

- 共同点：都重视基础知识、思维能力和实际应用。
- Commonality: All value basic knowledge, thinking skills, and real-life application.
- 差异：对探究、创新、跨学科和表达能力的重视程度不同。
- Difference: Varying emphasis on inquiry, innovation, interdisciplinarity, and communication skills.
